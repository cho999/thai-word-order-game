About Jam Akson Thai - Learn Thai Consonants with Audio and Quizzes

What you will learn

Thai consonant shapes, sounds, example words, and short-vowel practice items.

How to play

Use study cards, listen to sounds, take quick quizzes, or match Thai letters with sound labels.

Target level

Beginner learners starting Thai script and sound recognition.

Writing and romanization system

Thai script with Latin sound labels, IPA hints, and audio where available.

Available modes

Match game, learning mode, unit selection, sound checks, and quick quizzes.
