About Akson Thai - Thai Alphabet Memory Game

What you will learn

Thai consonants and vowels, paired with their Paiboon-style readings.

How to play

Flip cards and match each Thai letter with the correct reading card.

Target level

Beginner learners who are building recognition of Thai script.

Writing and romanization system

Thai script with Paiboon-style romanization and sound hints.

Available modes

Consonants, vowels, single-level practice, cumulative practice, and adjustable pair counts.
